## The top ten algorithms in data mining

### 分类(Classification)

- C4.5
- CART
- K Nearest Neighbours KNN
- Naive Bayes

### 统计学习(Statistical Learning)

- SVM
- EM

### 关联分析(Association Analysis)

- Apriori
- FP-Tree

### 链接挖掘(Link Mining)

- PageRank
- HITS

### 聚类(Clustering)

- K-Means
- BIRCH

### 袋装与推进(Bagging and Boosting)

- AdaBoost

### 序列模式(Sequential Patterns)

- GSP
- PrefixSpan

### 集成挖掘(Integrated Mining)

- CBA

### 粗糙集(Rough Sets)

- Finding reduct

### 图挖掘(Graph Mining)

- gSpan
